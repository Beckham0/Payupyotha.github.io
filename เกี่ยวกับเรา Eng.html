Payup Yotha Co., Ltd

Payup Yotha Co., Ltd.was established on December 11, 2008, operated by a team of highly experienced professionals in the construction of factories, warehouses, roads, commercial buildings, residential houses, and apartments.

We specialize in ready-mixed concrete production and distribution, offering comprehensive services that include expert consultation and project supervision, led by Mr. Payup Prasatthong, a dedicated executive who ensures quality at every stage of the construction process.

With over 10 years of proven quality and reliability, we have earned the trust of both government agencies and private sectors to deliver various construction projects continuously and successfully.
